India has reported nearly 49,000 new cases in the past 24 hours, taking the total tally of cases past 13 lakh. The country also registered 757 deaths, taking the total death toll from coronavirus to 31,358.

Out of 13,36,861 infections recorded so far, over 4.5 lakh are active cases while 8,49,432 patients have recovered. It may be noted that the country has been recording over 40,000 cases since July 23.
